# i-lab Pilot Program — Intake Form ## Typeform Question Specs Copy each block into Typeform as a new question. Question type is noted for each. Suggested logic jumps are noted where relevant. --- ### WELCOME SCREEN **Title:** Welcome to the i-lab — let's get to know you **Description:** This takes about 3 minutes. Your answers help us make sure you get the most out of this program, and help us improve how we match students to the right resources over time. --- ### BLOCK 1 — About you --- **Q1 — Short text** What's your name? *Placeholder: First and last name* --- **Q2 — Short text** Which Harvard school or program are you part of? *Placeholder: e.g. HBS, HKS, SEAS, FAS, HMS…* --- **Q3 — Dropdown** What year are you in? - 1st year - 2nd year - 3rd year or beyond - Post-doc / fellow - Alumni - Staff / affiliate --- **Q4 — Multiple choice (single select)** Which program are you signing up for? - Customer Validation Primer - Customer Discovery Sprint *Note: Use this to filter responses by program in your spreadsheet later.* --- ### BLOCK 2 — Where you are right now --- **Q5 — Multiple choice (single select)** How would you describe where you are with your idea or venture right now? - 🔍 Exploring — I'm curious but don't have a defined idea yet - 🧪 Validating — I have an idea and I'm figuring out if it works - 🔧 Building — I'm actively developing a product or service - 📈 Scaling — I have traction and I'm working to grow *Button label: That's me* --- **Q6 — Multiple choice (single select)** What feels most stuck or unclear for you right now? - 🧭 Sense of direction — I'm not sure what to work on or how to start - 👥 Finding collaborators — I need co-founders, teammates, or early customers - 📚 Skills & knowledge — I need to learn specific things to move forward - 💰 Funding & resources — I need money, space, or tools to keep going --- **Q7 — Multiple choice (single select)** What domain is your work or interest closest to? - 💻 Technology — software, AI, hardware, data - 💚 Social impact — health equity, education, climate, civic - 🔬 Life sciences — biotech, medtech, therapeutics - 🎨 Creative & media — design, media, consumer, entertainment - 🤷 Not sure yet --- **Q8 — Multiple choice (single select)** How much time can you realistically commit each week right now? - A few hours — exploring on the side of coursework - 5–15 hours/week — serious side project energy - 20+ hours/week — this is my main focus - Full time — I've committed fully to this venture --- ### BLOCK 3 — Your experience with customers --- **Q9 — Multiple choice (single select)** Roughly how many conversations have you had with potential customers or users so far? - None yet - 1–5 - 6–15 - 16 or more --- **Q10 — Opinion scale (1–5)** I know who my target customer is. *Scale labels: 1 = Not at all · 5 = Very clearly* --- **Q11 — Opinion scale (1–5)** I feel confident talking to customers or potential users. *Scale labels: 1 = Not at all · 5 = Very confident* --- **Q12 — Opinion scale (1–5)** I know what I need to learn or validate next. *Scale labels: 1 = Not at all · 5 = Very clearly* --- ### BLOCK 4 — Your goals for this program --- **Q13 — Multiple choice (multi-select)** What do you most hope to get out of this program? Select all that apply. - A framework I can actually use - Confidence to go talk to customers - Help figuring out what to test first - Accountability to get moving - Peer feedback on my idea or approach - Connections to advisors or mentors - Just exploring — not sure yet --- **Q14 — Long text** In one or two sentences, what's the specific thing you want to make progress on? *Placeholder: e.g. "I want to figure out whether the problem I'm solving is real for my target customer" or "I have an idea but I'm not sure who I'm building it for."* *This field is optional but helps advisors prepare.* --- ### THANK YOU SCREEN **Title:** You're all set 🎉 **Description:** Thanks for taking the time — this really helps. We'll see you at the program. If you have questions before then, reach out to the i-lab team at ilab@harvard.edu. --- ## Typeform Setup Notes **Form name:** i-lab Pilot Intake — Summer 2026 **Notifications:** Set up an email notification to your team inbox on every submission. **Responses destination:** Connect to Google Sheets via Typeform's native integration (Connect → Google Sheets) — this creates a live spreadsheet that updates with every submission, ready for analysis at the end of the summer. **Logic jumps (optional):** - If Q4 = "Customer Validation Primer" → skip Q8 (time commitment is less relevant for a 1-session workshop) - Q14 is optional — mark as not required in Typeform settings **UTM / hidden fields (optional):** Add a hidden field for `source` if you're sharing the form via multiple channels (newsletter, Slack, advisor referral) — lets you track where participants are coming from.
