Program Fit Guide

Who benefits most from each offering

Customer Validation Primer 1 session

A hands-on workshop introducing frameworks for gathering and interpreting customer feedback

Fit for
  • Anyone with an idea, concept, prototype, or message to test
  • Founders collecting feedback that hasn't felt useful
  • Anyone at any stage who's never structured a feedback process
Best fit for
  • Founders about to talk to customers for the first time
  • Someone with confusing or contradictory feedback they can't act on
  • Sweet spot: "I have something to test but don't know how to do it well"
Not fit for
  • Founders already running structured discovery with a working process
  • Someone who hasn't yet defined what they're building or for whom
Customer Discovery Sprint 3 weeks

A structured sprint combining workshops, real interviews, peer reflection, and advisor feedback

Fit for
  • Early-stage founders with an idea and target customer but limited real-world testing
  • Teams preparing for accelerators or pitch competitions
  • Anyone who knows they should talk to customers but keeps not doing it
Best fit for
  • Founders who need accountability and structure to execute
  • Can commit 5–10 interviews over two weeks and wants peer feedback on findings
  • At problem/solution fit stage, sharpening value proposition before building
Not fit for
  • Founders with significant discovery done who are now in build mode
  • Anyone who can't realistically do 5+ interviews in two weeks
  • Someone without a narrowed problem space — they'd struggle to know who to talk to

Sequencing note: The Primer is essentially a prerequisite mindset for the Sprint. Someone who jumps into the Sprint without it may collect interviews but struggle to interpret them. Consider surfacing the Primer before or alongside the Sprint for anyone flagging low confidence in customer conversations.
